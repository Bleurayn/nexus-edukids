# COPPA & Child Data Safety

NEXUS EduKids is designed from day one to be fully COPPA, GDPR-K, and FERPA compliant.

- No persistent cookies or tracking of children under 13
- All generated content runs client-side or through verified safe AI
- Parental consent flow built into production roadmap
- Third-party audits scheduled Q1 2026

We treat child safety as a non-negotiable engineering requirement.
